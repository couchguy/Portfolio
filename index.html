Hi, my name is Dan Kass and I'm currently an Applications Development Engineer for CBRE. I am passionate working with new techonlogies and learning new skills.

During my day job I'm part of a team of engineers that analyzes Building Data Analytics. We take commercial buildings, from retail stores to skyscrapers, HVAC data and bring it into an application called SkySpark.We analyze the data to point out innefficienceis and help our customers save energy.

On the side, I'm a freelance web developer designing and building sites like this one for blogs and businesses. Check out out Basic Book Blog! I can also help with getting domain names and hosting websites. I use Google Domains and Amazon Web Services respectively.

My favorite hobby is home brewing beer. It's a great hobby that has so many different aspects involved. I get to do hands-on DIY projects; when it comes to the brewing equipment, and chemistry when it comes the brewing itself. I'm much better at brewing chemistry now than I ever was in school. Experimenting with different ingredients is also blast! It's crazy how changing something small can have a big affect on the beer. Having good beer is just an added bonus to everything else included in the hobby.
